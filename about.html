I’m going to cycle 1000km in a month.
I’ll record every trip
The money I won't be giving to TFL,
I'll use to build a new bike.

And the time I'll save, I will use to convince you to do it too.
Because cycling is awesome. And if I can do it, everyone can.

My name is Adeline and I'm not a professional cyclist.
I work full time somewhere in Central London.
This is my plan for May 2014.

How does it work ?

I'll try and cycle everywhere I go during the month of April 2014

I'll record my trips with Strava
and simulate the TFL equivalents
with Google Maps

If I manage to ride at least 1000Km,
I'll use the money saved to
build a new bike.

Why am I doing this?

Why do people take the tube? It's expensive, you're underground, cramed into a tin can with hundreds of other people, hidden from the greatest city in the world. On top of framing your day with grumpiness, I'm also almost sure it makes you loose your time. At least a little bit.

Not long ago, I moved to London. I thought my feet or TFL's infrastructure were the only ones who could take me places. And then I bought a bike. It's silly, but it completely changed my life. For the better.

All in a sudden, I wasn't late anymore. I understood my neigbourhood. My new city. I became fitter - even stopped smoking.

I deeply belive that if a bike improved my day to day life like this, it could improve yours too. And if the size of the smile on my face isn't enough to convince you, maybe those numbers will be.
Ok, you convinced me X
